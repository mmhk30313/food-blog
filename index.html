Recipe Of Plain Pudding:

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup of sugar
  • 2 cups of evaporated milk
  • 3 eggs

How to make:

  1. Take a mixing bowl
  2. Take 3 egg yolks in the bowl
  3. Pour the milk
  4. Add the sugar
  5. Mix it very well
  6. Pour the mixture into a cake tin
  7. Place it in the preheated oven
  8. Wait for 1hour
  9. Take the tin out from the oven
  10. Refrigerate it for 2 hours.
